Sketchfab BBCode and embed popup
Support site: http://sketchfab.com
Support email: support@sketchfab.com

----------
This mod will add a Sketchfab BBCode and button in the forum's text editor, allowing users to embed 3D models inside their posts.
Compatible for SMF v2.0+
